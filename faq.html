Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our GLP-1 nutrition therapy program

How is this program different from other nutrition services?

+

Unlike general nutrition services, our program is exclusively designed for the unique needs of GLP-1 medication users. We address specific challenges like appetite suppression, muscle preservation, and long-term sustainability that standard programs don't cover.

Our approach is tailored to your specific medication, dosage, and stage of treatment, with specialized protocols for optimizing body composition (not just weight loss) and preventing rebound weight gain. As Michigan's first dedicated GLP-1 nutrition practice, we offer a level of specialization you won't find in general weight loss programs or medical clinics.

What exactly is included in the 12-week program?

+

Our comprehensive 12-week program includes:

  • Initial comprehensive assessment and personalized nutrition plan
  • Six 1:1 coaching sessions with your Registered Dietitian (bi-weekly)
  • Unlimited app support between sessions for accountability and questions
  • Custom meal plans and nutrition strategies tailored to your specific GLP-1 medication, dosage, and stage
  • Specialized protocols for muscle preservation and optimal body composition
  • Comprehensive GLP-1 nutrition guide with strategies and troubleshooting tips
  • Curated grocery lists and meal ideas optimized for GLP-1 users
  • Restaurant and travel guide for maintaining your plan while dining out
  • Progress tracking system for monitoring key metrics beyond the scale
  • Transition and maintenance plan for long-term success

For more details, visit our Program page.

How much time will I need to commit each week?

+

The time commitment is designed to be manageable for busy professionals. You'll have a 45-minute coaching session every other week (6 total over the 12 weeks). Between sessions, you'll spend about 10-15 minutes daily logging your meals and checking in via our app.

Our meal plans and strategies are designed for simplicity and efficiency, not requiring extensive meal prep or complicated recipes unless you prefer that approach. Many clients find that the program actually saves them time by eliminating the guesswork around what to eat while on GLP-1 medications.

Are the sessions virtual or in-person?

+

All coaching sessions are conducted virtually via secure video conferencing, making our program accessible to clients throughout Michigan regardless of location. This virtual format provides maximum convenience while still delivering personalized, high-touch support.

Between sessions, you'll have continuous support through our dedicated client app, where you can message your dietitian, track your progress, and access your personalized resources.

What happens after the 12-week program ends?

+

By the end of the 12-week program, you'll have a comprehensive maintenance plan tailored to your specific situation, whether you're continuing medication long-term or planning to discontinue it.

Many clients find that they have the tools and strategies they need to continue independently. For those who desire ongoing support, we offer maintenance packages with monthly check-ins to ensure continued success and accountability.

We also have an alumni community where past clients can connect, share experiences, and access updated resources as the GLP-1 landscape evolves.

Do I need to be on a specific GLP-1 medication?

+

No, our program supports clients on all GLP-1 medications, including Ozempic, Wegovy, Mounjaro, Zepbound, Rybelsus, and others. We tailor our approach to your specific medication, dosage, and stage of treatment.

We also work with clients who are transitioning off these medications and need support maintaining their results.

What if I'm just starting my GLP-1 medication?

+

This is actually the ideal time to begin! Starting our program alongside your medication helps establish optimal nutrition habits from day one, preventing common pitfalls and maximizing your results from the beginning of your journey.

We can help you navigate the initial adjustment period, manage side effects through proper nutrition, and set up systems that will maximize your results as your dosage increases.

Do you prescribe GLP-1 medications?

+

No, we do not prescribe medications. As Registered Dietitian Nutritionists, we provide specialized nutrition therapy to support your GLP-1 medication journey, but the medication itself must be prescribed by your physician or a specialized prescribing service.

We're happy to collaborate with your prescribing provider to ensure a coordinated approach to your care. Many of our clients find that their providers appreciate the specialized nutrition support we provide, as it helps optimize medication effectiveness.

What if I stop my medication during the program?

+

Our program is specifically designed to be adaptable to changes in your medication status. If you discontinue your medication during the program (whether due to side effects, insurance issues, or personal choice), we'll adjust your nutrition strategy accordingly.

In fact, having expert guidance during the transition off medication is extremely valuable, as this is when many people experience challenges with appetite regulation and weight maintenance. We'll help you navigate this transition successfully with specialized strategies to maintain your results.

Is this covered by insurance?

+

Our concierge program is a premium service not typically covered by insurance. However, we provide superbills that you may submit to your insurance for possible reimbursement, and many clients use HSA/FSA accounts to cover the investment.

Some clients with out-of-network benefits or nutrition coverage for medical conditions (such as diabetes or pre-diabetes) may receive partial reimbursement, but this varies by insurance plan.

We offer payment plans to make the program more accessible, with options to spread the investment over the 12-week period.

Do you offer payment plans?

+

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans to make our program more accessible. You can choose to pay in full for a discount or spread payments over the 12-week program period.

Our standard payment plan includes an initial payment followed by two additional monthly payments. We can discuss these options during your free strategy call to find what works best for your situation.

Why invest in nutrition support when I'm already paying for medication?

+

This is an excellent question. While GLP-1 medications are powerful tools for weight management, research and clinical experience show that proper nutrition support significantly enhances results and helps prevent common pitfalls:

  • Without proper nutrition, many GLP-1 users lose valuable muscle mass along with fat, which compromises metabolism and increases the risk of weight regain
  • The reduced appetite from GLP-1 medications often leads to inadequate protein and nutrient intake, causing fatigue, hair loss, and other issues
  • Many patients regain weight after stopping medication or hitting plateaus without the right nutritional strategy

Think of our program as an investment in maximizing your medication's effectiveness and ensuring lasting results. Many clients view it as protection for their significant investment in GLP-1 medications, which can cost thousands of dollars annually.

What kind of results can I expect?

+

Results vary based on individual factors including your starting point, medication, dosage, adherence to the program, and metabolic factors. However, our clients typically experience:

  • Optimized fat loss while preserving valuable lean muscle mass
  • Improved energy levels and reduced GLP-1 side effects
  • Better metabolic health markers (blood sugar, cholesterol, etc.)
  • Development of sustainable nutrition habits that support long-term success
  • Increased confidence in maintaining results whether continuing or discontinuing medication

While we can't guarantee specific weight loss amounts, our approach is designed to ensure that the weight you do lose is primarily fat (not muscle), which is crucial for long-term success and metabolic health.

How quickly will I see results?

+

Many clients notice improvements in energy, digestion, and GLP-1 side effects within the first 1-2 weeks of implementing our nutrition strategies. Body composition changes typically become noticeable within the first month.

Our program is designed as a 12-week journey because this timeframe allows for meaningful, sustainable changes in both body composition and habits. We focus on steady, consistent progress rather than rapid results that are difficult to maintain.

Remember that the quality of your results (losing fat while preserving muscle) is just as important as the speed, especially for long-term success.

Will I regain weight after stopping GLP-1 medication?

+

This is a common concern, and research does show that without proper strategies, many people regain weight after discontinuing GLP-1 medications. However, our program is specifically designed to minimize this risk through several approaches:

  • Focusing on muscle preservation during weight loss, which maintains metabolic rate
  • Developing sustainable nutrition habits that can continue after medication
  • Creating a specific transition strategy for medication discontinuation
  • Establishing proper monitoring systems to catch and address small regains before they become significant

While we can't guarantee that no weight regain will occur, our clients who follow the transition protocol typically maintain the majority of their results or experience only minimal regain compared to those without specialized support.

Do I need to follow a specific diet like keto or intermittent fasting?

+

No, we don't require you to follow any specific diet trend. Our approach is personalized to your preferences, lifestyle, and what works best with your specific GLP-1 medication and dosage.

Rather than prescribing a one-size-fits-all diet, we focus on key nutritional principles that optimize GLP-1 results, such as adequate protein intake, nutrient density, and meal timing strategies. Within these principles, there's flexibility to accommodate your food preferences and lifestyle.

That said, if you already follow a specific dietary pattern (vegetarian, Mediterranean, etc.) that you enjoy, we can certainly work within that framework while optimizing it for your GLP-1 journey.
