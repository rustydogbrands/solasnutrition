Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if this program is right for me?

+

This program is specifically designed for adults who are currently using, about to start, or have recently discontinued GLP-1 medications (like Ozempic, Wegovy, Mounjaro, or Zepbound) and want to maximize their results. If you're looking for expert guidance to optimize fat loss, preserve muscle, and ensure lasting results, this program is for you. The best way to determine if it's a good fit is to book a free strategy call to discuss your specific situation.

Do I need to be on a specific GLP-1 medication?

+

No, our program supports clients on all GLP-1 medications, including Ozempic, Wegovy, Mounjaro, Zepbound, Rybelsus, and others. We tailor our approach to your specific medication, dosage, and stage of treatment. We also work with clients who are transitioning off these medications and need support maintaining their results.

Is this covered by insurance?

+

Our concierge program is a premium service not typically covered by insurance. However, we provide superbills that you may submit to your insurance for possible reimbursement, and many clients use HSA/FSA accounts to cover the investment. We also offer payment plans to make the program more accessible.
