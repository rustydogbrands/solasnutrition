About Solas Nutrition

Michigan's First Dedicated GLP-1 Nutrition Therapy Practice

Our Mission

At Solas Nutrition, we're on a mission to transform the GLP-1 medication experience through specialized nutrition therapy that maximizes results and ensures lasting success.

We believe that these powerful medications offer an unprecedented opportunity for sustainable weight management, but only when paired with expert nutritional guidance tailored to each stage of the GLP-1 journey.

Our name "Solas" comes from the Irish word for "light" or "illumination" – reflecting our commitment to bringing clarity, guidance, and a brighter future to those navigating the complex world of GLP-1 medications.

Meet Your Registered Dietitian

Expert GLP-1 Nutrition Specialist

Michelle Michael, RD, CEO & Founder

Registered Dietitian | GLP-1 Nutrition Specialist

As the founder of Solas Nutrition, I bring two decades of experience as a Registered Dietitian with specialized training in GLP-1 medication nutrition support, practicing since 2005.

My journey into GLP-1 specialization began when I witnessed firsthand the transformative potential of these medications – but also the struggles many patients faced without proper nutritional guidance. I saw clients losing precious muscle mass, experiencing nutritional deficiencies, and ultimately regaining weight after stopping medication.

This led me to develop the Solas SUSTAIN Program, a comprehensive nutrition therapy approach specifically designed for the unique needs of GLP-1 medication users at every stage of their journey.

I am a proud graduate of Western Michigan University and maintain active registration with the Commission on Dietetic Registration. I regularly participate in continuing education focused on the latest research in GLP-1 medications and metabolic health.

Our Philosophy

GLP-1 Medications Are Tools, Not Solutions

We believe that GLP-1 medications are powerful tools that create a window of opportunity for transformative change – but they are not complete solutions on their own. The right nutrition approach is essential to maximize their benefits and ensure lasting results.

Quality of Weight Loss Matters

Not all weight loss is equal. We focus on preserving lean muscle mass while targeting fat loss, which is crucial for maintaining metabolic health, physical function, and preventing weight regain.

Sustainable Habits Create Lasting Results

We emphasize building sustainable nutrition and lifestyle habits that support long-term success, whether you continue medication indefinitely or eventually discontinue it.

Personalization Is Essential

Each person's GLP-1 journey is unique. We tailor our approach to your specific medication, dosage, stage of treatment, metabolism, preferences, and goals.
