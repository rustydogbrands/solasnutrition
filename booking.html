Frequently Asked Questions

Is there any obligation to enroll after the call?

+

Absolutely not. The strategy call is completely free and comes with no obligation. It's simply an opportunity for us to understand your needs and for you to learn more about our approach.

How should I prepare for the call?

+

No special preparation is needed. It's helpful if you can share details about your current GLP-1 medication, dosage, and any specific challenges or goals you have, but we'll guide the conversation to ensure you get the most value from our time together.

What if I need to reschedule?

+

You can easily reschedule your call through the confirmation email you'll receive after booking. We ask that you provide at least 24 hours' notice if possible, but we understand that life happens and will accommodate changes as needed.
